Located in Pollachi, Tamil Nadu, Unicorn Coco Products specializes in exporting high-quality coconut-based products, with a focus on Coir Fibers and Coco Peat. We are committed to sustainable practices and delivering eco-friendly products to global markets.

Coir Fibers

Coir fiber, extracted from coconut husks, is strong and durable, used in ropes, mats, brushes, and more. Our coir fibers are biodegradable, high in tensile strength, and available in white and brown varieties, suitable for industrial and domestic use.

Coco Peat

Coco peat, or coir pith, is a natural by-product of coconut husks, widely used in horticulture and agriculture. It has excellent water retention properties, making it ideal for nurseries, greenhouses, and gardening. Our coco peat is 100% organic, eco-friendly, and available in blocks, briquettes, or loose form.
